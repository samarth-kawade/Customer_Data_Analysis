## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior across **10 shopping malls in Istanbul** spanning 2021 to 2023. The analysis covers demographic segmentation (gender, age), product category performance, payment method preferences, and mall-wise revenue distribution — all visualized through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Dashboard Pages

The Power BI dashboard is organized into **6 interactive pages**:

1. **Overview** — KPIs, revenue by category & mall, transaction trends by year/month
2. **Gender Analysis** — Transaction share, quantity, revenue, and category split by gender
3. **Age Group Analysis** — Shopping behavior across 18–30, 31–45, 46–60, and 60+ segments
4. **Category Analysis** — Revenue and quantity breakdown across all product categories
5. **Payment Analysis** — Distribution of Cash, Credit Card, and Debit Card usage
6. **Insights** — Consolidated key findings and cross-segment patterns

---

## Key Findings

- **Female customers** drive ~60% of transactions (59K) and revenue (₺150M vs ₺101M for males)
- **Clothing** is the top category with ₺114M revenue (45.33% share) and 104K units sold
- **31–45 age group** is the highest-spending segment at ₺73M revenue and 87K units
- **Technology** earns high revenue (₺58M) despite low unit volume (30K), indicating premium pricing
- **Cash** is the most preferred payment method across all demographics
- **Mall of Istanbul** and **Kanyon** are the top-performing malls, each generating ~₺51M
- The **60+ segment** is the most under-monetized despite representing 17.26% of customers
